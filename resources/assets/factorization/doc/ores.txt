\title Ores

Silver can be found in largish clumps below Y=46.
Both silver and lead can be extracted from it.
(It's actually Galena.) 
Silver is used as a reflecter, and lead is used as a conductor.

\p

Dark iron ore is an unusual metal.
It is found in craters in the bedrock,
and is clearly not the product of any natural process.
This material is uniquely suitable for for \link:servo servos\endlink and other automata.
